🌿 Outlook & Aspirations

🧰 Nature of Work

My internship at Mandai blended technology with conservation—designing bots to automate backend workflows, orchestrating video shoots from storyboard to delivery, and supporting hands-on animal care. The scope spanned scripting, fieldwork, and stakeholder engagement, teaching me how tech solutions can enhance sustainability.

🚧 Challenges Faced

From debugging flaky Power Automate flows to synchronizing GoPro setups under time constraints, each hurdle demanded resilience. Coordinating interviews across multiple departments also pushed me to level up my project management and communication skills.

📈 Learning & Growth

I expanded my tech toolkit—Power Apps, AI Builder, Power BI, OCR pipelines and prompt engineering became familiar pathways. Daily interactions with stakeholders improved my agile mindset, and over 200 certifications further anchored my knowledge in cloud, AI, and security.

🏆 Accomplishments

  • Produced & presented the Project 100K townhall video with professional shooting & editing workflow
  • Delivered three distinct Power Automate bots deployed by actual departments
  • Conducted in-depth GenAI tourism analysis, visualized via dashboards
  • Enhanced Park Ops through live field workflow mapping
  • Achieved 200+ certifications spanning AI, cloud, cybersecurity & beyond

🚀 Career Aspirations

I aspire to be a versatile engineer—melding RPA, AI, user empathy, and ethical design. I’m passionate about building tools for sustainability, conservation, education, and accessibility. The vision? Tech that empowers people and protects our planet.

🌟 Final Thoughts

This internship reaffirmed my purpose—merging automation with real-world impact. It's emboldened my confidence, empathy, and ambition. I’m excited to take these lessons forward, shaping future projects that are technically sound, user-centered, and purpose-driven.
