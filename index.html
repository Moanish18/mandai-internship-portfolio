My Internship Journey @ Mandai Wildlife Group

I'm thrilled to present my internship e-portfolio, which documents my first 16-week journey at Mandai Wildlife Group’s Transformation Office as a Digital Transformation Intern. This opportunity allowed me to immerse myself in real-world AI, automation, and digital media projects in a cross-functional workplace that values innovation.

Throughout this internship, I applied what I learned from my Applied AI diploma at Temasek Polytechnic to meaningful projects. I worked with tools like Power Automate, Power BI, Miro, GenAI platforms and even handled professional filming and video editing for the “Project 100K” initiative, which was showcased during a company-wide townhall.

I also got hands-on experience supporting field operations — from Zebra feeding sessions to observing Park Operation Department's workflows. These tasks helped me understand how automation can empower even the most on-ground processes.

My colleagues and supervisor constantly inspired me with their insights and feedback, helping me bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical industry application. This portfolio showcases the projects, skills, certifications, and reflections from my time at Mandai — a journey that’s shaped my growth as an aspiring RPA Engineer and AI Developer.
